A Brand That Grew with India

More than two decades back a path-breaking idea to disrupt the market took shape. Lemon Tree Hotels started this journey by introducing branded hotels in the midscale segment. With the launch of Lemon Tree Hotel, Udyog Vihar in 2004, the brand reimagined hospitality. Many other hospitality brands have since then followed the path of India’s most beloved homegrown hotel brand.

Today, Lemon Tree Hotels' vision stands tall in the form of one of the fastest-growing chains of hotels and resorts with over 100 properties in more than 65 vibrant destinations. The hospitality brand caters to a wide range of travellers with business and leisure hotels.

From the fast-paced heart of India’s business hubs to the tranquil charm of hill stations, laidback coastal escapes, and adventurous wildlife retreats, there’s a Lemon Tree Hotel for every kind of traveller. Whether you're on a work trip, a weekend getaway, or a family vacation, our hotels blend comfort, convenience, and character in every stay.

With time the story extended India’s borders. Starting with Dubai in 2019, our footprint now extends to Nepal and Bhutan where we welcome guests with our signature Indian warmth on global soil.

What has remained constant across all our properties are our thoughtfully designed spaces, heartfelt service, and a promise to be “Refreshingly Different”. Hospitality, for us, isn’t just about rooms — it’s about creating memorable moments, one stay at a time.

Looking for dependable, high-quality experiences without the frills? Lemon Tree Hotels, our midscale offering, is your go-to partner perfectly balancing value and hospitality for both business and leisure travellers.

Each brand under the Lemon Tree umbrella is unique, yet all share the same commitment — to make your stay memorable and meaningful.

Driven by Purpose: Sustainability and Inclusion

Hospitality at Lemon Tree Hotels extends beyond service to a heartfelt narrative of inclusion, compassion, and conscious living. Step into any of our 110+ hotels, and you are welcomed not just by our team in uniform, but by a four-legged colleague with soulful eyes and a wagging tail — a once-forgotten stray, now nurtured, named, loved and taken care of as part of our extended family. These K9 ambassadors are not just pets; they’re storytellers of second chances who spread joy and warmth.

The other part of our story is the inclusion of our team members who have overcome physical, intellectual, social, or economic barriers. Through our commitment to employing Opportunity Deprived Indians (ODIs), including Persons with Disabilities and individuals from marginalised communities, we’ve built a workforce rooted in empathy and equal opportunity.

We extend our care for people and paws to the planet by designing eco-conscious hotels that strive for LEED Gold standards by embracing sustainability at every step.
